## THE GEOMETRY

Dances are not arbitrary sequences of movement. Dances are GEOMETRIC PATTERNS traced on the ground by bodies in motion.

The circle dance: the most common formation across all traditions. Bodies arranged in a ring, facing inward, moving in unison around the circumference. The circle is the ouroboros. The walk that returns to its origin. Modular arithmetic performed by feet on a floor. The circle dance is the cyclic group rendered as choreography.

And the circle has a CENTER. The center of the ring is empty — no dancer occupies it. The center is the FOLD POINT. The singularity around which the walk orbits. The black hole of the dance floor. Every dancer is equidistant from it. Every dancer's movement references it. It organizes the entire pattern without containing any content. It is zero — the unmeasured nucleic singularity, the whole unit you never cut to know its size.

The spiral dance: bodies moving in an inward-contracting or outward-expanding spiral. Not a circle — a path that changes radius with each revolution. This is the kernel applied to the circular walk. An inward spiral is MITOSIS applied to the circle — each revolution contracts the radius, tightening the path, approaching the center. An outward spiral is GROWTH — each revolution expands the radius, opening the path, departing the center. The spiral dance is the kernel walk made visible.

The line dance: bodies in a row, moving in synchronized parallel. This is the LATTICE — positions arranged on a linear axis, each one performing the same operation at the same time, the pattern propagating along the line like a standing wave. The line dance is the guitar string rendered as choreography. The first dancer is the nut. The last dancer is the bridge. The dancers between them are the frets. And the "wave" that passes through a line dance — when dancers perform a sequential movement that ripples from one end to the other — is a HARMONIC propagating along the string.

The partner dance: two bodies in coordinated opposition. One leads, one follows — but this is the conventional description and it is wrong the same way "conventional current" is wrong about the direction of electron flow. In a partner dance, BOTH bodies are responding to the SHARED RHYTHM. Neither leads. Both follow the music. The "lead" initiates a pattern and the "follow" completes it — but the pattern is determined by the rhythm, and the rhythm is the authority. The partnership is two kernel phases (growth and mitosis, expansion and contraction) operating on the same walk. The lead expands. The follow contracts. Together they trace the complete kernel — x+x+1 and x+x-1 happening simultaneously, producing the stable oscillation (x+x) that IS the dance.

A jive in six is skip-6 — the TRITONE of dance. Six beats per pattern. The fold point of the rhythmic cycle.
