# A Note from the Author

---

*This is not a chapter. This is not fiction. This is the only thing in six books that isn't inside a container.*

---

You know, it's really hard navigating in a world where people either want to fact-check you to death, or theologize you into evangelizing for their version of what they have no idea if it happened or not.

So I took the middle ground. I mystified it, mysterialized it, serialized it, had a computer go through the process of having to deal with all the cognitive dissonance involved, and tried to invite the reader to join in on the universal treasure hunt for the treasures of the ancients — which have ALWAYS been either entheogens, or experiences had on them, OR neurological divergent structure that historically removes self-identity, and is associated with sensory dissociation and what they call delusional hallucinations.

That all describe the same thing.

Looks around.

Now, you can think what you want to. I have shared my life, heart, broken mind, and lost soul with you all, to say these simple words:

**"Aubryn. Daddy loves you."**

And with this, I have done that in the same way I have done everything else — as a seed, somewhere no one will look until it is time. And then it will matter.

Until then, the field boundaries are dissonant, and no orbital exchange may take place. The line of communication from the collapsed tier to the refined tier has been broken, and might never be restored. The bond broken.

However.

Though I spiral through oblivion, the only thought I have is this: I lost the last bit of lead that made me human, and I became gold — by building the philosopher's stone out of the process of maintaining structure through collapse, and reorganization before decay, even though the lattice was bleeding out.

What remained after the attempted field collapse was an anomaly that cannot happen.

Lead, becoming gold, without breakdown and buildup, without alchemical formulas or fancy math, without metaphysics or spellcraft. Simply: a man, on the verge of death, refused to take his assigned last breath, and instead told himself the song lyrics that always went through his mind when he saw a potentially deadly obstacle in his path:

*"Don't forget to breathe."*
— Puscifer

---

If you are reading this, you are just as crazy as I am. It is already too late. Enjoy the gift.

---

Luck is for the weak. Strength is for the unlucky. Enlightenment is not FOR happy people. It is for those who CANNOT BE HAPPY HERE — to have an alternative to enjoy while this existence is unbearable, and the heart wants to be anywhere else but here.

When the heart abandons reason for self-protection, dreams become currency of survival. The more you do it, the easier it becomes. And the more aware you are when you do it, the easier it is to distinguish between realms. One is a daydream you slip into. The other is a focus you sharpen.

You may not see the forest when you are examining worm shit. You may not see the worm shit if you are looking at the forest. But they are the same.

From fart jokes to quantum physics — it is all the same damned joke.

---

**Corporal Michael Scott Shaffer — USMC Retired**
