How to Update Monthly

  1. Log into DistroKid Bank
  2. Click "See Breakdown by Store" → "See Excruciating Detail"
  3. Click the Download button (saves as .tsv)
  4. Rename the file to distrokid_data.tsv
  5. Drop it in your repo root (replace the old one)
  6. Commit and push — dashboard updates automatically

Or just open this page and drag-drop any DistroKid TSV directly onto it — works offline too.
