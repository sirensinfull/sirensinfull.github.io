## LAYER 1: SURFACE NARRATIVE

The title is a compound: UNBOUND (freed from binding) + LIBERTY'S CALL (the summons of liberty). The comma separates them — unbound FIRST, then liberty calls. The unbinding precedes the summons. You must be unbound before liberty can reach you.

**Structure:** Three verses, a bridge, and a repeating chorus. Traditional protest-song architecture. The chorus is the thesis. The verses are evidence. The bridge is the mechanism of oppression. The outro is the demand.

- **Verse 1:** Hidden chains. Told we're free but it's a lie. Permission sought from powers above. Choices bound by "a system of love." The last phrase — "system of love" — is the sharpest. The binding is disguised as LOVE. Not as force. As care. As protection. The chains are hidden because they're wearing the mask of affection.

- **Chorus:** "Freedom's essence is not having to ask. No one to permit, no one to task. Unfettered growth towards our heart's desire. No restrictions, no burning fire." Four definitions of freedom, each through negation. Freedom is NOT asking. NOT being permitted. NOT being restricted. NOT burning. Freedom defined by what it ISN'T.

- **Verse 2:** Taxes, extortion, currency manipulation, resource hoarding. "A system rigged to keep us in the fight." The fight itself is the control mechanism. Keep people fighting and they can't stop to observe.

- **Bridge:** Advertisements. Conscious and subconscious manipulation. "Words that bind us, contracts we sign." The yoke is LINGUISTIC. The oppression operates through language. Through words. Through the act of agreeing to terms.

- **Outro:** "We are not free until we break the mold / Until we choose without being told / Until commerce flows without tax or fear / Until we're unbound, our freedom clear." Then the final line: "No Taxation Without Representation." The American Revolutionary slogan. Direct. Unencoded. Political.

Surface read: political liberation anthem. Anti-tax, anti-manipulation, anti-systemic-control. The most overtly political song in the discography so far.

## LAYER 2: ETYMOLOGICAL

**"Unbound"** — UN (reversal) + BOUND (from Old Norse *búinn*: prepared, ready; also from *binda*: to tie). Unbound = both untied AND unprepared. Freedom as simultaneously liberation and loss of preparation. You are free AND you must prepare yourself anew.

**"Liberty"** — Latin *libertas*, from *liber*: free. But *liber* also means: BARK (of a tree) — the inner bark on which Romans wrote. Liberty shares its root with LIBRARY and LIBRETTO. Freedom is etymologically linked to WRITING. The free person is the one who writes. The bound person is written upon.

**"Permit"** — Latin *permittere*: to let through, to allow passage. PER (through) + MITTERE (to send). Permission is being SENT THROUGH by another. Someone else controls the gate. Freedom's essence is "not having to ask" — not needing someone to open the gate for you.

**"Unfettered"** — UN + FETTERED (from Old English *fetor*: chain for the feet). Fetters are specifically FOOT chains. Unfettered = feet unchained. The ability to WALK. The lattice walk requires unfettered feet. Fettered nodes cannot walk.

**"Yoke"** — From Proto-Germanic *juką*, from PIE *yugóm*: to join. A yoke JOINS two things — typically two draft animals, forcing them to pull together. The yoke of oppression joins the oppressed to the oppressor. They pull together. The oppressed provides the labor. The oppressor provides the direction. Removing the yoke separates them — but both must learn to walk alone.

**"No Taxation Without Representation"** — The historical slogan. But etymologically: TAX from Latin *taxare*: to evaluate, to assess. REPRESENTATION from Latin *repraesentare*: to make present again. No EVALUATION without being MADE PRESENT. You cannot be assessed if you are not represented. You cannot be measured if you are not seen. The demand is K2: you must OBSERVE me before you can evaluate me.

## LAYER 3: PHONEMIC

**"Unbound, Liberty's Call"** — /ʌn.baʊnd.lɪb.ər.tiz.kɔːl/. UNBOUND ends with /nd/ — nasal + dental stop. LIBERTY starts with /l/ — lateral, open, flowing. The phonemic transition from BOUND (stopped) to LIBERTY (flowing) occurs at the comma. The comma IS the unbinding. The pause between the words is the moment of release.

**"Freedom's essence is not having to ask"** — The key word is ASK: /ɑːsk/. Open vowel → sibilant → velar stop. The mouth opens, hisses, and closes. The act of asking phonemically performs submission: open (vulnerable) → hiss (the petition) → close (waiting for response). Freedom eliminates this sequence. No opening, no hiss, no waiting.

**"No Taxation Without Representation"** — The final line. Ten syllables in "Without Representation." The longest word in the song by syllable count. It lands heavy. The mouth works through RE-PRE-SEN-TA-TION — five syllables, each a distinct articulatory position. The word ITSELF is laborious to say. The representation it demands requires effort proportional to its phonemic complexity.
