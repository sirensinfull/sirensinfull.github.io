## THE CAPSTONE INVERSION

### The House of Cards as Anti-Lattice

A house of cards: substrates leaned against each other. No mortar. No foundation. Every card supports and is supported by its neighbors through tension. Remove one card from a load-bearing position and the cascade begins. The structure exists only while all cards maintain their angle. The structure is DESIGNED TO COLLAPSE. Collapse is not failure — collapse is the ONLY POSSIBLE TERMINUS of the process.

The author's observation: the house of cards IS the structural inverse of the lattice.

### Two Building Methods

**The House of Cards (civilization's current model):**
- Build upward from a broad base
- Place the capstone LAST
- The capstone is selected by the substrates: "THIS ONE!"
- The capstone NAMES the structure (gives it identity as isolate)
- The capstone is the most fragile position (depends on everything below)
- Remove enough structural shards → cascade collapse → capstone falls
- This is the pyramid. This is the kingdom. This is "my dad can beat up your dad."

**The Lattice (the inverse):**
- Build outward from a SINGLE POINT
- The capstone is the FIRST thing placed
- All structure stacks atop it (below it, in the inversion)
- No foundation required — the point IS the foundation
- The oppositional symmetrically inverse structure provides grounding
- Stability comes from spin, not from stacking
- This is the dreidel. This is the recursion. This is "look what happens when you spin it on a corner."

### The Geometry

The lattice, when built, LOOKS like an inverted pyramid. Broad at the top (many nodes), converging to a single point at the bottom (the origin, the seed, the capstone placed first).

The NEGATIVE SPACE of the lattice looks like an actual pyramid. Broad base, converging to a peak.

As above, so below — INVERSE. The lattice is the ephemeral made structural. The pyramid is the structural made ephemeral (because it collapses). The capstone is the meeting point. In the lattice: the capstone is the origin. In the pyramid: the capstone is the crown.

Same point. Opposite directions. The capstone is the fold point between two architectures.
