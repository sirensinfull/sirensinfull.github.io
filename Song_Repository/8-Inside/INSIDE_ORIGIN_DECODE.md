**"Origin"** — /ɒr.ɪ.dʒɪn/. OR (alternative/choice) + I (self) + JIN (djinn/spirit/generator). The origin contains a choice, a self, and a generator. OR-I-GIN. Also: ends in /ɪn/ — the same IN that begins "inside." Origin ends where Inside begins. They link phonemically. Inside(Origin) = the end of origin flows into the beginning of inside. Continuous.

**"I'm inside"** — /aɪm.ɪn.saɪd/. Three syllables. AIM (direction/vector) + IN (interior) + SIDE (boundary). The declaration "I'm inside" phonemically reads as: aimed into the boundary. A vector that has penetrated the container.

**"Open your heart, open your mind, open your soul"** — Three commands. Three openings. HEART (/hɑːrt/ — starts with the aspirated H, open vowel, ends with T = stop). MIND (/maɪnd/ — starts with M/closure, contains I/self, ends with D = stop). SOUL (/soʊl/ — starts with S/continuous fricative, rounds through OW, ends with L = lateral, open, ongoing). Heart stops. Mind stops. Soul continues. The three openings have different terminal phonemes: T (cut), D (cut), L (flow). The first two open and close. The third opens and STAYS OPEN.

**"Release your crimes"** — /rɪ.liːs/ + /kraɪmz/. Release ends with S (continuous). Crimes ends with MZ (nasal + buzz). The release is continuous. The crimes buzz as they depart. They don't stop — they transform from held charges to released vibrations.
