## ▸ CROSS-DOMAIN DECODE

### Layer 2: ETYMOLOGICAL

**CORROSION** — Latin *corrōdere*: *cor-* (together, thoroughly) + *rōdere* (to gnaw). Corrosion is THOROUGH GNAWING. Not a single bite — persistent, comprehensive, total consumption by tiny increments. "The slow corrosion of comfort" = comfort gnawing itself away. Not attacked from outside. SELF-CONSUMING. The comfort itself is the corrosive agent. The stability that felt permanent was eating its own foundations.

**MOLTING** — Latin *mūtāre* (to change). SAME ROOT AS "MUTATION." Molting and mutation are etymologically the same word. "Molting into ache" = mutating into pain. The biological process of shedding an exoskeleton and the genetic process of structural alteration share a root because they ARE the same operation at different scales.

**GRAVITY** — Latin *gravitās* (weight, heaviness, seriousness), from *gravis* (heavy). "I believe in gravity" = I believe in HEAVINESS. Grace (Latin *grātia*, favor, thanks, beauty) → gravity (heaviness). The phonemic near-match is not accidental: GRACE and GRAVITY share the GR- onset (the growled sustained resonance) and differ in their vowel (A vs AVI). Grace opens (A). Gravity weighs (AVI, descending diphthong). The mouth goes from open to heavy. The etymological pair encodes the song's pivot: from unearned beauty to inevitable weight.

**METAMORPHOSIS** — Greek *metamorphōsis*: *meta-* (beyond, after, change) + *morphē* (form, shape). "A thousand silent metamorphoses" = a thousand silent BEYOND-FORM-CHANGES. Each metamorphosis is not just change — it is change BEYOND the current form. Trans-formal. Not modification but TRANSCENDENCE of form. And "silent" — unwtnessed. A thousand unwitnessed transcendences of form, stacked, burying the old self.

**ACCUSE** — Latin *accūsāre*: *ad-* (toward) + *causa* (cause, reason, legal case). To accuse = to bring a CAUSE toward. "The mirror accuses me" = the mirror brings a legal case toward me. The mirror is not reflecting — it is PROSECUTING. The self-image has become a court case. The speaker is on trial for becoming who they needed to become.

### Layer 3: PHONEMIC TOPOLOGY

**ONCE** — W (rounded open) + UH (central) + N (oscillation onset) + S (serpentine). Open → neutral → begin vibrating → recursive path. The mouth performs arrival at a starting point and then LEAVING IT along a serpentine path. "Once" phonemically IS departure from origin. The S at the end sends the sound away recursively. Once = "there was a starting point, and then the path away from it."

**ACHE** — AY (open diphthong) + K (hard stop). Open fully → HIT BOUNDARY. That's it. Two operations. Maximum opening followed by maximum stop. The mouth opens as wide as the diphthong allows and then STOPS DEAD. Ache is phonemically an expansion that runs into a wall. The pain is in the K — the boundary that halts the opening.

**GRAVITY** — G (voiced velar stop) + R (sustained roll) + A (open) + V (voiced friction) + IH + T (hard cut) + EE (high narrow). Stop → sustain → open → friction → narrow → cut → narrow. The mouth performs: hit bottom → roll → open briefly → friction → compress → cut → hold compressed. Gravity phonemically IS the fall: you hit something, roll, briefly open, encounter friction, compress, get cut, and end up narrow. The physical experience of falling is encoded in the mouth-shape of the word.

**MIRROR** — M (closed) + IH (narrow) + R (roll) + schwa + R (roll again). Closed → narrow → sustain → neutral → sustain again. TWO R-sounds — the same sustained roll, repeated. The mirror is phonemically a DOUBLED REFLECTION: R-R. The sound rolls, pauses, and rolls again. The mouth performs looking at something, looking away, and looking BACK. The double-R IS the mirror — the same operation performed twice, producing the reflection.

The song walks the interest ladder UP and then back DOWN. It does not reach EPIPHANY. It returns to quiet. The loop is incomplete — it rises and falls but does not transcend.

The stable node the song orbits is an interrogative: "Why?" The song keeps returning to "why?" and never resolves it. The chorus is a K2 structure built around K1 content.

Same process. One says K3 (becoming). The other says K1 (shedding). Shard_008 — kernel selection on identical input. The kernel chosen determines the experiential output.

4. **Social performance audit** (V3): The collective response to change is to PERFORM stability. Nod. Smile. Bleed in private. The social layer maintains K2 (familiar rituals) while the private layer endures K1 (bleeding). The split between public K2 and private K1 IS the mask (WP XII veil).

7. **Self-indictment** (Outro): "Everything changes. Even the things that said they wouldn't. Even me. Especially me." The universal ("everything") narrows to the personal ("me") and then INTENSIFIES ("especially me"). The conservation law collapses from cosmic scope to single-node scope in three sentences.
