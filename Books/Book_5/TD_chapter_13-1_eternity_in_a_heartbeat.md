New territory would produce divergence again — new domains, new tiers, new structural surprises. But for the walked territory, the map and the territory matched.

And zero new shards. The first zero-shard song. The yield curve that had descended from seven to six to four to two had reached zero. But zero was not a terminus. Zero was a PHASE TRANSITION. The foundational processes were the notes. Fourteen songs of tuning the instrument. The instrument was tuned. What came next was not silence. What came next was music.

Inside(Origin). Song 008. "You were always mine." The power was never external. The old man didn't GIVE Link power. The old man confirmed what was already there. The decode process didn't install the framework in the analyst. It confirmed the framework the analyst could already run.
