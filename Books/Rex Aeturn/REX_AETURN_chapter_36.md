## THE INFERNO: NINE CIRCLES

The descent through Hell is a descent through an inverted cone — a pyramid flipped upside down, its base at the surface of the Earth and its tip at the center. Each circle is narrower than the one above. Each circle contains sinners sorted by TYPE OF SIN, with the severity increasing as the circle narrows.

The nine circles, read as lattice operations:

**Circle 1 — Limbo**: the unbaptized, the virtuous pagans. Those who never entered the walk. Not punished — simply absent. They exist at the surface of the structure without descending into it. Position zero of the inverted walk.

**Circles 2 through 5 — Incontinence**: lust, gluttony, greed, wrath. Four circles of EXCESS — the growth kernel running without restraint. Too much desire. Too much consumption. Too much accumulation. Too much anger. Each one is a specific mode of uncontrolled expansion, the growth kernel (+1) applied repeatedly without the mitosis kernel (-1) to balance it. Cancer in four variants.

**Circle 6 — Heresy**: the questioning of institutional doctrine. Note the placement: Dante — who was himself exiled from Florence for political heresy — places heresy BELOW incontinence. The institution's moral framework ranks questioning the framework as MORE SINFUL than uncontrolled excess. The machine protects itself more aggressively than it protects its operators. This is not Dante's error. This is Dante ENCODING the institutional bias into the poem so precisely that it reads as satire to anyone who can see the structure.

**Circle 7 — Violence**: against others, against self, against God and nature. The mitosis kernel WEAPONIZED. Destruction without the fold point that would convert the destruction into productive mitosis. The crosswise cut without the closure that heals. Violence is fission without reassembly — Set without Isis.

**Circle 8 — Fraud**: the stable kernel CORRUPTED. Presenting false structure as real. The counterfeit coin. The false prophet. The flatterer who reflects a distorted image. Fraud is not excess (growth) or destruction (mitosis) but DECEPTION — the stable kernel, which should preserve truth, deployed to preserve falsehood. The number line pretending to be the lattice. The continuous approximation claiming to be the foundation.

**Circle 9 — Treachery**: the deepest circle. Betrayal of bonds. Of kin, of country, of guests, of benefactors. The ANTI-UBUNTU. The severance of the relationship between node and molecule — the destruction of the thing that makes individual nodes into connected structure. Treachery does not misuse a kernel. Treachery destroys the BOND TOPOLOGY that the kernels operate on. Without bonds, there is no lattice. Without the lattice, there are no kernels. Treachery is the deepest sin because it doesn't just misuse the structure — it destroys the structure's CAPACITY to be used.

At the center of the ninth circle: Satan. Frozen in ice. Immobile. Enormous. THREE HEADS, each chewing a sinner — Judas (betrayer of the divine bond), Brutus (betrayer of the political bond), Cassius (betrayer of the social bond). Three mouths chewing three betrayers. Eternally. Without swallowing. Without resolving. Without producing anything.

The ternary kernel — growth, stable, mitosis — FROZEN. Not operating. Not walking. Not producing output. Three operations locked in stasis, each one consuming a broken bond, each one masticating endlessly without the cycle completing, without the fold point being reached, without the walk advancing one single step. Satan is the anti-walk. The anti-singularity. Maximum structure (enormous, complex, powerful) with zero motion (frozen, immobile, stuck). The lattice at absolute zero. The three kernels present but inoperative. The machinery of creation rendered static by the frozen state.

Nine circles. One inverted pyramid. The Fool descends through the tiers of kernel misuse to the center of the Earth where the frozen ternary sits in ice and the walk appears to have ended.

## THE PURGATORIO: SEVEN TERRACES

But the walk has not ended. Dante and Virgil pass THROUGH Satan (through the center, through the fold point, through the singularity at the tip of the inverted pyramid) and emerge on the other side — in the Southern Hemisphere, at the base of a mountain that rises from the ocean. The Mountain of Purgatory.

Seven terraces. SEVEN. The septaxial prime. Each terrace purges one of the seven deadly sins — the same sins that populate the circles of Hell, but here they are being CORRECTED rather than punished. The souls on each terrace are not suffering for their sins. They are SHEDDING their sins. Each terrace removes one layer of accumulated kernel misuse. The ascent through Purgatory is the reverse of the descent through Hell: where Hell compresses and freezes, Purgatory releases and ascends.

The seven terraces are the seven Mersenne tiers of the lattice: EXIST, SENSE, INTEREST, DECIDE, ACT, REMEMBER, FUSE. Each terrace corresponds to a tier, and the purging of each sin opens the next tier for ascent. The walk UP Purgatory is the walk THROUGH the cognitive alignment — from mere existence, through perception, through evaluation, through decision, through action, through memory, through integration.

At the summit: the Earthly Paradise. The Garden of Eden. The state that existed before the fall — before the crosswise cuts, before the detuning, before the suppression. The state where the lattice is accessible, the walk is unimpeded, and the human being stands at the top of the septaxial structure ready to ascend into the celestial spheres.
