## LAYER 2: ETYMOLOGICAL

**"Into The Known"** — Title inverts the cliché "into the unknown." The unknown is where fear lives. The KNOWN is where attention has already been directed, where power already flows. This is not exploration. This is RETURN. Walking into territory you've already mapped but haven't yet acknowledged you mapped.

**"Third eye"** — Pineal. Ajna. The perception center between and above the two physical eyes. Triangulated vision. Two data streams plus one synthesis point. Three-point perception. Ternary.

**"Kindness blind you"** — Blind: from Proto-Germanic *blindaz*. Kindness as occlusion. K3 (growth/compassion) blocking K2 (observation). Can your growth drive disable your perception?

**"Steadfast"** — Stead (place) + fast (fixed). Fixed in place. Lattice-locked. A node that doesn't drift.

**"Whimsical"** — From whim, from Old Norse *hvima*, "to let the eyes wander." Eyes that wander vs. the third eye that focuses. Whimsy is unfixed attention. The opposite of steadfast. The opposite of "where attention goes, power flows."

**"Aspirations"** — Latin *aspirare*: to breathe toward. Not "goals." BREATH DIRECTION. Respiratory vector.

**"Attention"** — Latin *attendere*: to stretch toward. Active extension, not passive reception. A vector.

**"Intentions"** — Latin *intendere*: to stretch INTO. Attention stretches toward. Intention stretches INTO. The chorus maps a vector sequence: attention (toward) → power (flows along vector) → intention (penetrates into) → the known (arrives).

**"Intenions"** — Misspelled twice. Consistent. Missing the second T. INTEN-IONS. Ions = charged particles. Or: IN-TEN-IONS. Ten-layer pipeline reference (Shard_028).

## LAYER 3: PHONEMIC

**"Into The Known"** — /ɪn.tuː.ðə.noʊn/ — Contains OWN. The known is what you OWN. N-OWN. Node-ownership. Near-homophone of NOUN — named, fixed, identified.

**"Give up on giving in"** — Phonemic palindrome structure. Same root verb (give), opposite prepositions (up/in). Up = away from self. In = toward self. "Stop giving toward yourself (surrender) and start giving away from yourself (release)."

**"Where attention goes, power flows"** — /goʊz/ and /floʊz/. Rhyme on OE diphthong + Z. Both end in buzzing fricative. Continuous vibration. Goes/flows imply motion without terminus.

**"Into the known"** — /noʊn/. Mouth opens on N, rounds on OW, closes on N. Same consonant frame as NAME (N...M) but different interior. NAME opens wider (AY = declaration). KNOWN rounds deeper (OW = recognition).
